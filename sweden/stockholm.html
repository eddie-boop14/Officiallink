🚆 Day Trips & Also Visit
from Stockholm
Uppsala
38 min by SJ train from Stockholm C. Sweden's oldest university city — cathedral, botanical gardens, Viking burial mounds at Gamla Uppsala.
🚆 38 min
Gothenburg
3 hr by SJ high-speed train. Sweden's second city — Liseberg park, maritime museums, Haga district, archipelago ferries.
🚆 3 hr
Malmö
4.5 hr by SJ train or 1 hr flight. Turning Torso, Malmöhus Castle, bridge to Copenhagen. Skåne region gateway.
🚆 4.5 hr
Visby, Gotland
3 hr by Destination Gotland ferry from Nynäshamn or 35 min flight. UNESCO-listed medieval walled town on Baltic island.
⛴ 3 hr
Kiruna & Swedish Lapland
1.5 hr flight or 17 hr night train. Northern Lights, ICEHOTEL, Abisko National Park, midnight sun.
✈ 1.5 hr
West Coast
Bohus coast via Gothenburg. Fishing villages, seafood, island-hopping on Kosteröarna, Marstrand fortress.
🚆 via Gothenburg
