About

The Hajar mountain range stretches across the northern and eastern UAE, shared with Oman, forming the country&#x27;s geological backbone. Peaks reach nearly 2,000 metres at Jebel Jais in Ras Al Khaimah. The mountains contain scattered villages, ancient terraced agriculture, copper mines dating to the Bronze Age, and wadi systems that fill with water after rain. Organised hiking trails have been developed in recent years, particularly around Jebel Jais, Wadi Shawka, Wadi Showka, and the Hatta area.
