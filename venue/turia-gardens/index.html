About

Europe's longest urban park from diverted river. 9km of gardens, sports, playgrounds, orange groves. Connects City of Arts to historic center.

Free. Allow 1-3 hours. A natural escape in the Valencia area offering trails, green spaces, and opportunities to experience local flora and fauna.

Walking paths and trails of varying difficulty make this accessible for families, casual walkers, and more serious hikers. Seasonal changes bring different character — spring wildflowers, summer shade, autumn colours, and winter tranquillity each offer a distinct experience.
