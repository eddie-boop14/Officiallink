About

AboutConsistently ranked among Brazil's best urban beaches, this sheltered crescent of sand sits within All Saints' Bay rather than facing the open Atlantic, producing calm, warm waters ideal for swimming.

The beach is framed by the colonial-era Forte de Santo Antônio da Barra (Farol da Barra), a 17th-century fortress housing a nautical museum and one of the oldest lighthouses in the Americas.
