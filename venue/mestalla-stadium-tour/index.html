About

Mestalla is the home stadium of Valencia Club de Fútbol, inaugurated on 20 May 1923 on its current site in the Eixample district of Valencia. With a capacity of 49,430, it is one of the largest and oldest continuously used football stadiums in Spain. The self-guided tour, branded the Mestalla Forever Tour, runs year-round on days when there are no match or event conflicts.

The tour route covers the press room, the home and away dressing rooms, the players' tunnel, the pitch level, the chapel, the mixed zone, and the club's dedicated museum areas including the Mestalla Per Sempre exhibition. Audio accompaniment is provided in multiple languages as guides lead groups through the stadium's operational areas. A separate school visit programme is available for educational groups at €9.00 per pupil.

Mestalla is located at Avenida de Suecia s/n, 46010 Valencia. The stadium is served by metro Line 5 (Aragón stop) and Line 3 (Facultats stop), and by city buses 10, 32, 71 and 80. Visitors with reduced mobility cannot complete the full route; an alternative accessible itinerary covering the pitch via hydraulic platform, dressing room, chapel, and balcony areas is available with advance reservation by email to the tour team.

FAQ
How much does the Mestalla Stadium Tour cost?
Adult (13–65): €16.10. Reduced (children 5–12, seniors 65+, pensioners, disabled, unemployed): €13.50. VCF members: €9.00. Children under 5: free. Book at entradastourmestalla.valenciacf.com. Prices valid until 30 September 2026.
Where do I buy official tickets?
Official tickets are sold exclusively at entradastourmestalla.valenciacf.com. Do not buy from GetYourGuide, Viator, or other resellers — they charge significantly more for the exact same timed entry slot.
What are the opening hours?
Mon–Sat: morning sessions at 10:45, 12:00, and 13:15; afternoon sessions at 15:45 and 17:00. Sundays: morning sessions only (10:45, 12:00, 13:15). Tours may be suspended on match days and for other operational reasons. Always check the current schedule at valenciacf.com/vcf-tour-mestalla before booking.
Is the tour accessible for wheelchair users?
Visitors with reduced mobility cannot complete the full standard route. An alternative accessible itinerary covers the pitch via hydraulic platform, home dressing room, chapel, mixed zone, Mestalla Per Sempre exhibition, Balcón de la Afición and Records Blanquinegres area. Prior reservation is required — contact the tour team in advance.
Is there a discount at the official VCF store?
Yes. Presenting your Mestalla Forever Tour ticket at the official Valencia CF store at Mestalla on the day of your visit gives a 5% discount on all purchases.
