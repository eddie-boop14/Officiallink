The Problem
Reseller sites charge 2–5× the official price for attraction tickets. A tourist searches "Vatican tickets" and pays €54 on a reseller — the official price is €17. This happens millions of times a year, across thousands of attractions worldwide. Most travellers never find the venue's own booking page. officiallink.org lists the real link for every attraction, but 3,524 venues still need their official source verified. That's where you come in.

Quick Reference — What Counts as Official?
✓ The venue's own domain (e.g. louvre.fr, britishmuseum.org)
✓ Municipal/government portals (e.g. paris.fr, kultur.gov.tr)
✓ National heritage bodies (e.g. english-heritage.org.uk, monuments-nationaux.fr)
✓ Venue-authorized ticketing (the venue links to it from their own site)
✗ GetYourGuide, Viator, Tiqets, FareHarbor — these are resellers
✗ Any site that adds "service fees" or "convenience charges" above the venue price
✗ Domains like "officialtickets-[venuename].com" — often fakes
