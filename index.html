Why This Exists

I paid €428 for what I thought was the official Vatican tour from the official web page, only to realise it was a reseller site built to look official — same title, same layout — just to deceive tourists like me and charge extra. I got the tickets through mail 12 days later clearly showing the real value. They took €294 extra and added no value apart from the stress of never receiving the real tickets.

This happens every single day to tourists worldwide. Vatican, Eiffel Tower, Colosseum, Sagrada Familia. Attraction tickets, visa applications, museum passes. Resellers dominate through ads and SEO tricks, appearing relevant and official just to profit from travellers who don't know better.

I got tired of it. So I built this directory. For myself first — and the idea of becoming the official linking platform was just a step from here.
