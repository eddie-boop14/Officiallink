Cairn de Gavrinis
Neolithic passage tomb on a small island in the Golfe du Morbihan, c.3500 BC. The finest megalithic engravings in Europe cover 29 of the 52 corridor stones.
from €12.00
✅ verified →
🌐 gavrinis.bzh📍 Maps

Fortifications de Vauban UNESCO
17th-century coastal fortifications by Sébastien Vauban across Brittany. Sites include Saint-Malo walls, Fort National, and Île Longue. Several are free to explore.
from €6.00

Golfe du Morbihan
Inland sea with 40+ islands and islets connected by tidal currents. Oyster beds, bird reserves, and neolithic monuments. Boat tours depart from Vannes and Auray.
from €18.00

Pointe du Raz
France's westernmost cape in Finistère. Grand Site de France. Dramatic granite headland above the Raz de Sein tidal race. Coastal walking path is free.
