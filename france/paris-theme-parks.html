35-hectare park with Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie, Géode IMAX dome, open-air concerts, and free green space. Most attractions ticketed separately.

Botanical garden and leisure park in the Bois de Vincennes. Seasonal flower displays, children's activities, summer concerts, and open-air theatre.

Zoo · Bois de Vincennes, 12th arr.
Paris's main zoo — redesigned and reopened 2014 with immersive biozones across 14.5 hectares. Giant pandas, lions, penguins, giraffes.
